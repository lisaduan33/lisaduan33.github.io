Welcome to Cook and Go! We have made this website to showcase our international backgrounds through food. All three of us are from different countries: Ireland, Canada and Korea. In today’s fast-paced society, it is sometimes difficult to find time to create satisfying meals and snacks. The recipes introduced in this website are recipes we believe are worthy of sharing because they are not only very quick to make, but also have cultural significance.











Sinéad O'Brien

I am a Psychology PhD student who is always on the look out for easy, quick recipes. I love cooking, baking and eating! If you're like me and you enjoy trying food from different countries this website is the place for you! Enjoy :)

Lisa Duan

I am a PhD student in Psychology and a devoted food-lover. I love coming-up with easy-to-cook food with all sorts of ingredients and sampling food from all over the world. Wherever there is food, there is me! Please enjoy with me some of the classic Canadian dishes I have selected!

Katrina Chu

I am a Land Economy student who love ice cream! I appreciate simple, quick and easy meals (but maybe this is because I'm lazy too) and my part-time hobby is eating around different street market foods. Hope you enjoy my selected quick Korean snack and side dish recipes!
